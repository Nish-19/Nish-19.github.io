## Research Projects

### Misinformation Detection

* Proposed a novel Deep Neural architecture modeling Misinformation Detection tasks using "Novelty" and "Emotion" of the fake-news data.
* Proposed a novel multi-tasking deep neural network architecture with Novelty and Emotion as auxiliary tasks to Fake-News Detection.
* Generated novelty and emotion aware embeddings for the final classification of fake news.
* Achieved significant improvement in classification accuracy on benchmark datasets like 7.92% on ByteDance, 17% on Covid-Stance, and 2% on the FNC dataset.
* Different versions of our paper are submitted to prestigious conferences and journals.

### Reddit Drug Abuse Detection

* Guide: Dr. Joydeep Chandra, Data Analytics Lab, IIT Patna.
* Investigated Drug Abuse on Social Media through Reddit Website.
* Collected and annotated huge data from Reddit on Drug Abuse.
* Built a multi-head attention deep neural network for classifying comments as motivational or not.
* The model also classifies the comments into sub-categories of altruism, hope, good advice, bad advice, and universality.

### Document Level Novelty Detection

* Proposed a Question-Answering based framework using GRU and Dynamic Memory Networks.
* Procured huge data from stack-overflow, organized, and pre-processed it.
* Built an annotation interface for annotation of documents on the sentence level.
